Macan (Persian:) is a Persian pop band in Iran. Amir Maghare (born 24 December 1995, Tehran) and Roham Hadian (born 29 July 1991, Arak) are the band's lead vocalists.[1] Yashar Khosravi, a composer and one of the most prominent saxophonists, took over the leadership of this band in 2016, after forming this band, and Amir Milad Nikzad is also the arranger of this band. The band was able to achieve great fame and popularity among music audiences shortly after its formation.
